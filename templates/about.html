About Rez.ai

Our Mission

Rez.ai helps job seekers optimize their resumes for specific job applications using artificial intelligence. Our goal is to increase your chances of getting interviews by ensuring your resume highlights the most relevant skills and experiences for each position.

How It Works

  1. Upload your resume - We accept PDF, DOCX, and TXT formats.
  2. Provide a job description - Either paste the text or provide a URL to the job posting.
  3. AI analysis - Our AI analyzes both your resume and the job requirements.
  4. Tailored result - Get a professionally tailored resume that emphasizes relevant skills and experience.

Privacy & Security

We take your privacy seriously. Your resume data is only used for the tailoring process and is not stored permanently on our servers. All uploads are automatically deleted after processing.
