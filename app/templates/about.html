About Rez.ai

Our Mission

Rez.ai helps job seekers optimize their resumes for specific job applications using artificial intelligence.

How It Works

  1. Upload your resume - We accept PDF, DOCX, and TXT formats.
  2. Provide a job description - Either paste the text or provide a URL.
  3. AI analysis - Our AI analyzes both your resume and the job requirements.
  4. Tailored result - Get a professionally tailored resume.
